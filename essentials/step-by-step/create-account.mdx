To begin using Plutus, you'll need to first create an account and generate your unique API key. Follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Visit the Plutus website and click on the ‘Sign Up’ button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verification">
    Check your email for a verification link from Vorticity. Click the link to enter your preferred payment method and verify your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate API Key">
    After verification, you will receive a second email containing your uniquely generated API key. **Make sure to write this key down or save it.**
  </Step>
</Steps>
